Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

SniFit (hereinafter referred to as "the App") takes user privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information Collection

This App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data.

All screenshot operations are performed locally on your device. Screenshot content is never uploaded to any server or cloud service.

Permissions

This App requires the following system permissions to function properly:

  • Screen Recording Permission: Used to capture screen content. This permission is only used when you actively initiate a screenshot. Captured images are saved locally or to clipboard and are never transmitted externally.

Data Storage

  • Screenshot files: Saved to your chosen local directory or clipboard
  • App settings: Stored locally (hotkeys, ratio preferences, etc.)
  • No cloud storage services are used
  • No analytics or tracking code is included

Third-Party Services

This App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page, and we recommend checking periodically.
